**Purpose**

The parent Enterprise Services ticket includes DAST validation as part of the overall delivery; however, execution is currently dependent upon completion of the required Acunetix project configuration.

* Confirm the project is ready for DAST execution.
* Communicate completion so validation activities can proceed.

Once the configuration has been completed, kindly notify the DevSecOps team so DAST validation can commence.
