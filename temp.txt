Proxy / Firewall Allow List Justification

Justification:

As part of the Acunetix On-Premises upgrade process, outbound HTTPS connectivity is required from the Acunetix Production server to Acunetix/Invicti update and service endpoints.

The same connectivity requirements were validated and tested successfully within the UAT environment. During UAT testing, the upgrade process initially failed due to missing outbound access to required Acunetix/Invicti service endpoints. Once the required proxy destinations were permitted, the update workflow was able to proceed successfully.

The following destinations are required to support product updates, licensing validation, telemetry communication, dependency retrieval, and related Acunetix services:

* updates.acunetix.com
* telemetry.invicti.com
* erp.acunetix.com
* *.amazonaws.com

Protocol:

* HTTPS (TCP/443)

Direction:

* Outbound only

Purpose:

* Acunetix software updates
* Dependency retrieval
* Product telemetry and health communication
* Licensing and update validation

This request is based on successful UAT validation and is required to support the planned Acunetix Production upgrade activity.


Network Service Check / SSL Inspection Bypass Justification

Justification:

During Acunetix UAT upgrade testing, outbound connectivity to required Acunetix/Invicti service endpoints was successfully established; however, update operations continued to fail due to TLS certificate validation errors.

Application logs confirmed certificate verification failures when connecting to Acunetix update services, specifically indicating:

* SSL_CERTIFICATE_VERIFY_FAILED
* unable to get local issuer certificate
* self-signed certificate in certificate chain

Investigation determined that HTTPS/TLS inspection applied to the outbound traffic was causing Acunetix to reject the presented certificate chain during update validation.

Following exclusion of the required Acunetix/Invicti endpoints from SSL/TLS inspection within UAT, update functionality completed successfully and validation scans were executed without issue.

Request:

Please exclude the following destinations from SSL/TLS inspection/decryption for outbound traffic originating from the Acunetix Production server:

* updates.acunetix.com
* telemetry.invicti.com
* erp.acunetix.com
* *.amazonaws.com

Reason:

Acunetix performs strict certificate validation against the remote service certificate chain. SSL/TLS inspection modifies the certificate presented to the application, resulting in certificate validation failures and preventing successful update operations.

This request is based on successful UAT validation and is required to support the planned Production upgrade.
